Control Center gives you command over a fleet of AI coding agents. Spawn agents, watch them work in real time, review their PRs, and manage repos, workspaces, and integrations — all from a native desktop app.

## What you can do

- **Dispatch agents** to isolated Git worktrees — they code, review, and open PRs in parallel
- **Review and merge PRs** with a built-in diff viewer, inline comments, and AI-powered review
- **Automate workflows** with DAG-based pipelines triggered by events or schedules
- **Manage knowledge** with workspace-scoped memory, facts, and policies
- **Track work** with vendor-agnostic tickets, projects, and delegation
- **Integrate** with GitHub, Linear, and 70+ MCP tools
